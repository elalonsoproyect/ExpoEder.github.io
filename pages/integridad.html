Cause and Effect Diagram Influencing Factors

The cause and effect diagram is built using these 5 factors:

1.The environment: the environmental conditions that affect the process are time, temperature, humidity and cleanliness.
2.Human factor: refers to anyone throughout the supply chain who assists in the process, including all support functions.
3.Method: establishes how the process is carried out and what requirements are necessary, such as quality procedures, work orders, instructions or drawings.
4.Machinery: includes all the machines and equipment required to perform a specific task.
5.Materials: raw materials, purchased materials, and everything that constitutes the manufacture of a final product.
